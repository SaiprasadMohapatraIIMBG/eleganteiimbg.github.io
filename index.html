About Us

IIM Bodh Gaya is a constituent of the prestigious and premier IIM brand, which symbolizes top-notch management education and contributes to the holistic development of individuals. Being a constituent of IIMs, it envisages the reputation of IIMs and the importance of management education in and around Bihar. Despite being a relatively new institute, it is gradually evolving into a revered name amongst the aspirants of management education in our country. Our institute comprehensively promotes academic rigor, intellectual discipline, and extracurricular activities. We envisage bringing back the glory during the days of the Nalanda. We also believe that contributing to society is as imperative as academic achievement. As responsible future managers, it is a prerogative of our students to contribute to society–having been actively conducting daily services quality assessment for Gaya district administration during the Pitrapaksha Mela, plantation drives, etc. We have also visited nearby villages, discussing and providing suggestions for the problems faced by the villages. Our annual cultural fest saw immense active participation from all IIMs and other prestigious management schools and other engineering colleges in 2019. Aspiring to uphold our standards and to strive towards holistic development, we are proud to announce our fifth annual management, cultural and sports fest, Eleganté 5.0

Elegante 5.0

Elegante 5.0 is an annual fest organized by the talented pool of students of the Indian Institute of Management Bodh Gaya. IIM Bodh Gaya’s annual fest is an attempt to reinforce its existing principles and foray further into the field of cooperation, relationship strengthening, and overall development of the society by bringing together students from different fields and from the different parts of the country.

Sports Events

Elegante creates enthusiasm and excitement by promoting the importance of sports through its various sports events. It awakens the zeal and fervor among students by giving them an opportunity to showcase their talent through exciting gaming challenges. It promotes improved coordination and quicker decision-making by helping players to unleash their potential and march into the gaming world.

Management Events

Elegante, a three-day event jam-packed with excitement, gives you a stage to show off your expertise, an arena to gawk at talent, or simply an excuse to come out and have a good time. It offers you a real-world learning opportunity through various management events on data science, strategy, human resource management, finance, operations, marketing, and many more.

Cultural Events

Elegante exposes students to an array of exciting activities along with academics. It strikes the cords of our hearts and provides melodies for every part of the emotional spectrum through phenomonal musical and dance performances . It invites students to unleash their creativity and design that masterpiece without any fear. Our comedy night will make you go crazy with laughter and take all your stress away.
