About Us

IIM Bodh Gaya is a constituent of the prestigious and premier IIM brand, which symbolizes top-notch management education and contributes to the holistic development of individuals. Being a constituent of IIMs, it envisages the reputation of IIMs and the importance of management education in and around Bihar. Despite being a relatively new institute, it is gradually evolving into a revered name amongst the aspirants of management education in our country. Our institute comprehensively promotes academic rigor, intellectual discipline, and extracurricular activities. We envisage bringing back the glory during the days of the Nalanda. We also believe that contributing to society is as imperative as academic achievement. As responsible future managers, it is a prerogative of our students to contribute to society–having been actively conducting daily services quality assessment for Gaya district administration during the Pitrapaksha Mela, plantation drives, etc. We have also visited nearby villages, discussing and providing suggestions for the problems faced by the villages. Our annual cultural fest saw immense active participation from all IIMs and other prestigious management schools and other engineering colleges in 2019. Aspiring to uphold our standards and to strive towards holistic development, we are proud to announce our fifth annual management, cultural and sports fest, Eleganté 5.0
